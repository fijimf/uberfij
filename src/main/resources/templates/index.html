Welcome to Deep Fij

Deep Fij is your one stop shop for half-assed college basketball data, statistics and analysis.

Data

We keep the last 10 seasons of game results. Nothing special, just games, dates and scores. We scrape the current season in not quiote realtime.

Statistics

We generate some pretty unsophisticated rudimentary stats from those scores.

Analysis

We try to use the latest in AI/ML opensource technology to analyze and predict the current season.
